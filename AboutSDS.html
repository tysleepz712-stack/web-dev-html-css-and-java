About SDS Driving School

Welcome to SDS Driving School, where our commitment to safer driving is at the forefront of everything we do. Established in 1993 by Tony Skerrit, who has been the sole owner and operator since its inception, our school has been dedicated to providing high-quality driving education for over three decades. At SDS Driving School, we believe that safe driving is not just a skill but a vital life lesson. Our experienced instructors, led by Tony Skerrit, bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom and behind the wheel. We offer personalized instruction tailored to each student's needs, ensuring that every learner gains the confidence and skills necessary to navigate the roads safely. Our curriculum covers everything from basic driving techniques to advanced defensive driving strategies, all designed to prepare our students for a lifetime of safe driving. We take pride in our positive, supportive learning environment, where students can feel comfortable and empowered to ask questions and practice their skills. Join us at SDS Driving School, where we're not just teaching you how to drive; we're instilling a lifelong commitment to safety on the road. Whether you're a new driver or looking to improve your skills, we are here to guide you every step of the way. Let us help you take the first step towards becoming a safer, more confident driver!
